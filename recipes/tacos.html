Birria Taco Recipe

Description

Birria tacos are easily my favorite food to make. They take some effort to make properly, but it is worth it. This is not a meal you can make for the family in 20 minutes after getting off work, so you need to be able to dedicate at least a few hours to make it right.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Ancho Chiles
  • Guajillo Chiles
  • New Mexico Chiles
  • Large Yellow Onion
  • 3 Roma Tomatoes
  • 3-4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 3 Bay Leaves
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Ground Cumin
  • Mexican Oregano
  • Ground Coriander
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  • Pinch of Ground Cloves
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1/2 Tbs White Vinegar
  • 3lbs Chuck Roast
  • White or Yellow Corn Tortillas
  • Oaxaca Cheese
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
